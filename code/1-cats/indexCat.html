Our website is your one-stop shop for information about cats. This page describes our many cat breeds. We've included photos of our cat breeds for your reference. Enjoy!

Cat Breeds

There are many cat breeds in the world. Researchers estimate that there are over 500 cat breeds in existence today. On average, a new cat breed emerges once every three years. This is an incredible time to be a cat lover!

We house the following cats at our shelter.

  • Abyssinian
  • Aegean
  • American Curl
  • American Bobtail
  • American Shorthair
